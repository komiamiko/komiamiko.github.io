Better Ordinal Game

Game Information

Zero ordinal is free and has no effect, but you must click for it at first.

Successor ordinals can be crafted using 3 of its predecessor. This cost increases for each that you have. Successors do the same thing as their predecessor, but take actions 3 times as fast.

Limit ordinals can be crafted using the first 4 ordinals from its fundamental sequence. This cost increases for each that you have. Limit ordinals will automatically craft ordinals strictly less than itself.

Once you reach certain ordinals, you can research a theory. You will lose all your ordinals, but receive a permanent upgrade to help you climb faster and go higher next time. In-game theories are only loosely related to real-world theories, and the upgrades conferred are rather unrelated.

ψ here is the extended Buchholz's psi function. With the original Buchholz's psi function, the limit would be ψ0(εΩω+1), but by relaxing the restriction on the cardinality, the new limit is ψ0(Λ), where Λ is the omega fixed point. Fundamental sequences are standard, except where a sub-expression has uncountable cofinality, in which case we use the usual iterated function trick.
